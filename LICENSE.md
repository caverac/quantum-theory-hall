## Additional Notes

### Educational Use

This project contains solutions to problems from *Quantum Theory for Mathematicians* by Brian C. Hall (Springer, 2013). The solutions are provided for educational purposes and to facilitate learning in quantum mechanics and mathematical physics.

### Academic Integrity

If you are a student using these solutions:

1. **Check your institution's academic integrity policies** before using these materials
2. **Use these solutions as learning aids**, not as direct submissions for coursework
3. **Understand the derivations** rather than copying results
